Presence and Attendance Fix Summary

Fixed:
- Team Availability now uses a real activity timeout instead of trusting old isOnline=true values.
- Users are only Available/Busy/Break when they have a fresh heartbeat/login activity.
- Stale users are moved to Offline automatically in the UI.
- Admins still appear in Available when they are truly online.
- Admins do not show Free since.
- Non-admin Available users show Free since.
- Offline users show Last seen with date and time.
- Team Attendance now lists all approved non-admin users, not only users with attendance logs.
- Team Attendance shows Online for active users and Last seen date/time for offline users.
- Backend JSON seed/state was cleaned so stale online users do not initially appear as available.

Important:
- Keep your working backend/.env file locally. It is excluded from this ZIP for safety because it contains your Gmail App Password.
- After replacing files, restart frontend and backend.
