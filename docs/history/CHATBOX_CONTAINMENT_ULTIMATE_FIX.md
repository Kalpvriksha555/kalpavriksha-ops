# Chatbox Containment Focused Fix

Only chatbox layout/attachment containment was changed.

What was fixed:
- Chatbox panel is constrained to viewport size.
- Large image/video attachments cannot expand the chatbox outside its boundary.
- Message body remains scrollable.
- Input bar remains fixed at bottom of chatbox.
- Direct message sidebar stays visible and no longer gets pushed away by attachment previews.
- Emoji/input section remains inside the chatbox.

No other business modules were changed.
